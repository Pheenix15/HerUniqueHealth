About Us

HerUniqueHealth Consult is dedicated to women’s health and education. In partnership with Fertility Education and Medical Management (FEMM), we provide a comprehensive health program for women of all ages who want to take charge of their wellbeing. We also collaborate with healthcare professionals across Nigeria to raise awareness about women’s health through community engagement. To date, we’ve hosted regular health classes and offered free medical outreaches in rural and suburban areas of Enugu State, Edo State, and the Federal Capital Territory. HerUniqueHealth Consult was founded by Dr.Ngozi Amakeze and Engr.Chinwe Odimmegwa and was formally registered with Nigerian Corporate Affairs Commission in 2021.

We believe that the heart of healthcare is service to others.
Our offerings include:

Comprehensive Women's Health Education

We’re here to help you understand your body better. Whether you're trying to learn more about reproductive health, hormones, or everyday wellness, we break things down in a way that makes sense—so you can feel more confident in your choices.

Medical Consultations (Including Telehealth)

You don’t have to figure things out on your own. Our medical consultations—both in-person and online—give you the chance to speak with a professional who genuinely listens and cares. We're here to help you find answers and support your wellbeing.

Medical Outreaches In Rural And Suburban Communities

We know that not everyone has easy access to healthcare, and that’s why we bring it to you. Through our medical outreach program, we’ve been able to reach women in communities across Enugu, Edo, and Abuja—offering checkups, education, and support right where it's needed most.

Seminars And Webinars On Women’s Health

We create safe, open spaces where you can learn about topics that matter to you—whether it’s fertility, menstrual health, or overall wellbeing. Join us online or in person, ask questions, and connect with others on the same journey.
